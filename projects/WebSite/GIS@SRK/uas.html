GIS @ SRK

Data Management solutions through harnessing the Web and the power of GIS

SRK Consulting uses unmanned aircraft system (UAS) technology to deliver enriched aerial mapping and imagery.

Adam Radel & Warren Medernach of SRK have developed a fixed-wing UAS platform for capturing aerial imagery at a fraction of the cost of other commercial UAS systems and traditional aerial photography.

By combining their technical expertise, RC knowledge, and off-the-shelf components, they have built a platform that will compete on par with commercially available offerings in the final product output while lowering operating costs.

The UAS technology can capture high quality images with horizontal ground resolutions of up to four centimeters. It can also capture elevation surface data with vertical ground accuracy of up to 10 cm in bare ground areas. This level of resolution and accuracy yields important details missed by other imaging platforms and approaches.

SRK's GIS experts can then use this image data to produce better quality, more informative topographical contour maps and 3-D digital surface models. The process is cost effective, quicker, safe to deploy, and the data and image outputs integrate easily with other GIS and CAD systems.
